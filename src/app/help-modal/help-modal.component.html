What is this app?

Hey there! This app is all about making Twitter Bingo super simple to create. If you're not familiar, Twitter Bingo is a fun game that's pretty popular among Twitter users. You create a Bingo and then your followers can play it by reposting your image with their results. Easy peasy!

How to use

Hey there! So you want to know how to use the app, right? 🤔 It's super simple! The app lets you easily create a Twitter Bingo. To get started, just add your first bingo cell and give it a title. 💡

Creating a new cell is a breeze. Just click on the "Add" button (+) on the bottom left side of your screen, add some text, and hit ✔️.

If you need to make changes, just click on a cell in the table and you'll be able to edit or delete it. The 🗑️ button will delete the cell and the cross button will cancel the changes. Easy peasy! 💪

To take a picture of your bingo, here's what you need to do:

  • Desktop: use scissors to crop the bingo.
  • Mobile: just take a screenshot 📱

That's it! Have fun creating your bingo. 🎉
